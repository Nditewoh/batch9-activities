Lee Ji Eun (IU)


Stage Name: IU
Full Name: LEE JI EUN
Date of Birth: May 16, 1993

IU (아이유) is a South Korean singer-songwriter and actress currently under EDAM Entertainment. She debuted on September 18, 2008 with a stage on M Countdown for the song "Lost Child".
Her stage name is derived from the phrase "I and You", symbolizing that people can become one through music.

Fun Facts and Trivia

IU is one of the most influential female celebrities in South Korea. She is one of the highest earners and has received numerous awards for her acting and music.

IU was a trainee for Good Entertainment with Uee, Yubin from Wonder Girls, Heo Ga-yoon from 4Minute, and Jun Hyoseong from Secret.

IU’s family struggled financially. She and her brother lived with her grandmother and cousins in conditions of poverty and apart from her parents for over a year during her middle school.

Age trivia: IU was 15 years old (15 international age; 16 Korean age) when she debuted as a soloist in 2008.

Height trivia: IU’s height is 162 cm (5'4''). Other kpop idols with the same height are: Jisoo (Blackpink), Jihyo (Twice), and Dara (2NE1).

Ideal type: IU used to say her ideal type was Taeyang (Big Bang). Some famous kpop idols that have listed IU as their ideal type or are known to be her biggest fanboys are: Jungkook (BTS), Yoseob (Highlight), actor Kim Soo Hyun, Suho and Kai (EXO).

Friendship: IU is very good friends with Suzy (Miss A), Jiyeon (T-ara), actress Yoo In Na, Sulli (fx), and Goo Hara (Kara)

Career Timeline

Year Age Career Event
2008 16 IU made her solo debut with the single Lost Child. Her first album was not successful.
2009 17 IU launched her first studio album and promoted the new single Boo, a huge style shift from Lost Child. IU started gaining more favorable attention.
2010 18 IU released the song Nagging with 2AM’s Lim Seul Ong. The song was one of the theme songs for the show We Got Married season 2.
2011 19 IU was part of the main cast of the variety show Heroes. She also made her acting debut in the drama Dream High.
2012 20 IU was recognized by Forbes Korea as one of the most influential people in South Korea.
2013 21 IU was the lead actress in the drama You Are the Best!
2014 22 IU won the 2014 KBS Drama Best New Actress award.
2016 24 IU acted as the female lead in Moon Lovers: Scarlet Heart Ryeo
2019 27 IU was the female lead in the drama Hotel del Luna.
